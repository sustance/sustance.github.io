## 3. Communications & AI Mesh

**Architecture**
- High-latency, low-bandwidth interplanetary network.  
- Data type: **Intent Objects** (procedural contracts, not raw packets).  
- Shared foundation: all nodes train on the same **simulation model + legal-ops library** (“Library of Congress” assumption).  
- AI autonomy level: Semi-autonomous; local decision-making within shared intent boundaries.

**Core Concepts**
- Predictive prefetching; protocol hardening.  
- Quantum key distribution for secure comms (QKD).  
- Periodic *library syncs* to align reference models.

**Failure Modes**
- Desynchronization after solar flare.  
- Misinterpretation of intent object → catastrophic event.  
- Human override impossible in short term due to light-speed delay.

**Scientific Challenges**
- Clock drift and relativistic timing differentials.  
- Radiation noise and hardware degradation.  
- neuromorphic nodes, swarm intelligence citations
