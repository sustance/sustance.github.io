- Scale: how broadly the event affects humanity;
- Impact: how strong the immediate effect is;
- Novelty: how unique and unexpected is the event;
- Potential: how likely it is to shape the future;
- Legacy: how likely it is to be considered a turning point in history or a major milestone;
- Positivity: how positive is the event;
- Credibility: how trustworthy and reliable is the source.
The factors are then combined into a single significance score and normalized to a 0-10 scale.
