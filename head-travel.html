Visas

Taiwan -- Visa-Free Entry Requirements for Australian Citizens:
  • Passport: > 6 months validity from the date of entry.
  • Purpose of Visit: Tourism or business.
  • Stay Duration: Up to 90 days.
  • Return/Onward Ticket: Confirmed return or onward air/sea ticket.
  • No Criminal Record: Must not have a criminal record.
  • No Emergency Passport: Entry is not permitted on an emergency passport.
