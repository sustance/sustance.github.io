Your anonymous listing of what you have or want will be linked to a unique code for matching in a social media group (WhatsApp). Do not provide personal identifying or location information in any public listing form fields. To physically exchanging goods do not communicate in the chat group use your private privately chat of mail. We will try to recruit commmunity, charity etc locations for anonymous dropoff or pickup can be done just using the listing codes. This system is not for money or harvesting your data. We never want know who you are and hopefully you dont ever need to know your counterparty.

Offer Laptop/Mini PC

Do you have a physically functional laptop or mini PC that is currently unusable, often because its proprietary Windows or Mac OS is no longer supported or upgradable? We invite you to list it here to connect with volunteers who can upgrade it with a free open source operating system like Ubuntu, XFCE, or FreeBSD.

We can not warehouse, stock or deliver machines. Volunteers will need to hold them in their own home so ancillary equipment (Screens, keyboards etc.) and large devices are excluded for now. Ss small devices and their power supply only!. Not Phones or Pads.
