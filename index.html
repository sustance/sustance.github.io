Sustance Matching

Connecting surplus computing equipment with volunteers for open source upgrades and subsequent donation to individuals or charities.

Your listing will be linked to a unique anonymous code for matching in our social media groups (e.g., WhatsApp). Please maintain your anonymity by not sharing personal contact details here. All further communication happens privately between matched parties.

Offer Laptop/Mini PC

Do you have a physically functional laptop or mini PC that is currently unusable, often because its proprietary Windows or Mac OS is no longer supported or upgradable? We invite you to list it here to connect with volunteers who can upgrade it with a free open source operating system like Ubuntu, XFCE, or FreeBSD.

We will not warehouse, stock or deliver machines. Volunteers will need to hold them in their own home so ancillary equipment (Screens, keyboards etc.) large devices are generally excluded. Small devices and power block only!. Not Phones or Pads.

Sponsors & Identity

Participants who wish to be identified may provide their details and we will manually list them on the web site and associate them with their unique code. Paid sponsors may advertise here to help cover costs for parts and freight.

Disclaimers

  • All work is expected to be done for free and voluntarily. Donations are welcome but not required.
  • Once contact is established between recipient and volunteer, any arrangements for freight, parts, or time are private agreements.
  • Abuse of goodwill will result in removal of your listing or registration.
  • Multi-pass disk overwrites are recommended but no guarantee exists that sensitive data remnants are fully removed.
  • Electrical safety remains the responsibility of the recipient.
  • Open source licenses apply as allowed for this activity.
  • Please do not provide personal identifying information in any public listing or form fields.
